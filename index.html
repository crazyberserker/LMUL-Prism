The
Project

Are you ready to give color to your life?
Do you want more color in your life?

Prism is an application where you control a day in a person's life and the decisions you make could break the routine he/she is trapped in and bring colors into this person’s journey. The narrative begins with an introduction made by a narrator, who explains the initial situation of monotony and routine in the character's life. The whole image is in shades of gray. A standard day begins, with small choices to be made through an interactive interface. Each choice may or may not bring color to the character's world. Our goal with this project is to show people who are stuck in their routines, and people who think they do not have control over their lives that every decision matters. No matter how small a decision is, it has an impact on our lives, and we are, in fact, in control. The idea behind this project is to show that small choices in our daily routine that seem insignificant, can bring color into our world and improve life quality. In the end, we will collect the data from the decisions each user makes and compare it with every other person that interacted with Prism. This will offer the opportunity for people to compare their decisions and reflect on their choices. With this, we want to encourage people who are stuck in a routine to make different choices in their daily life. People can improve their life quality, even if by a little, making life a little more colorful, joyful and captivating.
